About TransformIT

TransformIT is a $8 billion leading global technology and IT enterprise company listed in the US. Founded in 1982, we are one of USA’s original IT garage start-ups. A pioneer of modern computing, TransformIT is a global transformational enterprise today. Its range of offerings includes product engineering, custom & package applications, IT infrastructure services, systems integration with products across a wide range of focused industry verticals. Our team consists of over 90,000 professionals of diverse nationalities, who operate from 31 countries including over 500 points of presence in the US.

Key Service Offerings

  1. Security & Network Services,
  2. End-user Computing,
  3. Datacenter & Mainframe services,
  4. Integrated Operations Management service,
  5. Cross Functional services,
  6. Application Operation Services,
  7. Cloud Computing Services.

Our major competitors in this domain have been the likes of some of the top IT vendors in the world such as IBM, HP-EDS, Accenture, CSC and also include some of the top emerging players in this field such as Wipro, Capgemini, Xerox, TCS, Infosys. We have a huge success rate against these competitors when it comes to acquiring some of the most eminent Fortune 100 customers.

TransformIT is the fastest growing line of business of HCL Technologies. An end to end owner of IT Infrastructure through differentiated value proposition “Industrialized IT Management”. The concept of Industrialization is truly based with cloud computing as one of the platform of delivering that service. What the customer gets is optimized; Flexible, Centralized & Standardized & business aligned IT.

One of our biggest competitors against which we have gained momentum in the recent years is IBM. For the last 100 years, it has been termed as the biggest innovator in IT, Computer Hardware products and R&D services. It’s services has ranged from Business services to IT services to Consulting and Product designing. TransformIT has changed the paradigm of IT services from its legacy model, where IT was operated as a black box to the customer to a new and modernized approach where the customers get an unusual flexibility in choosing their vendors for different services. TransformIT has delivered total transparency to all its customers with innovations such as CIO Dashboard. As a result, the customers enjoy a full-fledged view of their IT.

TransformIT’s Differentiating Edge

  1. Trust, Transparency and Flexibility for customer to choose whichever vendor they prefer for services,
  2. Option for Selective Outsourcing where customer doesn’t want total outsourcing,
  3. Partnered with top 3 Fortune 500 Server, Network and End User product OEMs to give our customers an unusual flexibility,
  4. 100% Customer Contract Renewal Rate,
  5. A strong partner model to ensure flexibility and realize cost savings,
  6. 24x7 operations, covering 17 languages, Delivery centers across world,
  7. Proven tool based transition framework,
  8. Tool-as-a-Service – Integrated performance, visibility experience for infrastructure landscape.
