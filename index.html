Welcome to TransformIT

TransformIT was one of the earliest companies in the world to recognise and adopt Remote Infrastructure Management and Co-sourcing as a primary delivery model for delivering Infrastructure management services. We have been consistently recognised by analysts and customers alike as “Thought Leaders” in this space. TranformIT has been rated as No.1 in Traditional Outsourcing Space and also in Remote Management Infrastructure Outsourcing by Datamonitor’s The Black Book of Outsourcing 2009-10.

Coming from a background of running stock exchanges we understand the criticality and know what a minute in operations means. We are the only US Offshore IT Services provider who has a dedicated Infrastructure focus and completely understand the different between the “RUN IT” and “DEVELOP/BUILD IT”. TransformIT’s approach hence is completely governed from an ITIL perspective rather than approaching the same from a CMMI perspective. TransformIT was the first company to establish Integrated Operations Center.

TransformIT has been delivering connectivity services for more than 15 years and has evolved its network business at different stages of technology evolution. Some of the key milestones as listed below emphasis the fact that Connectivity services is the Core DNA of our Infrastructure Services division.

TransformIT set up the largest VSAT installed base of more than 14000 terminals across 15 hubs – Introduced C and KU band in the country.

TransformIT was one of the early entrant in to Internet Service Provider Business in India having set up POP across the country.

TransformIT set up dedicated R&D facility for CISCO, contributed to more than 49 patents for CISCO R&D team. TransformIT has more than 1800 engineers working for CISCO R&D.

TransformIT has been pioneer of NOC and SOC services and was one of the first Offshore Company to invest on these facilities. Currently NOC and SOC has combined staff of more than 2000 engineers.
